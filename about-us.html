About Us

Advancing social justice through education, creative expression, and inclusive economic initiatives.

Mission

To advance social justice and recognize the rights of marginalized communities through education, creative expression, and inclusive economic initiatives.

Legal Status

Registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882; holds 12A and 80G certification enabling donor tax benefits.

Our History

Established in 2016 by Ananya Rao, a social entrepreneur and cultural practitioner.

Milestone

Instrumental in community advocacy that contributed to policy dialogue surrounding the National Social Inclusion Framework (2021).
